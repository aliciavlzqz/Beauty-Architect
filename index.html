Get to know Lorena.

Her aim is to achieve the most natural results in all her services for all her clients.

Lorena is a certified Phibrows artist. She specializes in Permanent Makeup: including Powdered Eyebrows and Microblading. MICROBLADING

If you are looking to enhance your eyebrows, give them a fuller look, this semi-permanent procedure is the perfect choice.

Natural hair-like strokes are made, using a manual tattoo technique. Microblading is expected to last between one and three years. For most clients, a touch-up session is required about four to eight weeks after initial session. Results may vary, depending on client's skin condition.

Next

POWDERED EYEBROWS

Looking for an ON-POINT brow makeup look? This is the technique for you.

Powdered eyebrows can give you a full or ombre look. It creates a desired fullness in a bespoke arch shape that best suits your facial features and brings out your natural beauty. This technique can last between 2 - 3 years, after a touch-up session.
